# 🌿 GreenBit: AI-Powered Sustainability Dashboard

GreenBit is a comprehensive personal and industrial sustainability dashboard built to scale. Whether you are looking to minimize your household footprint or optimize industrial operations for a greener future, GreenBit provides the tools, data tracking, and AI intelligence to turn climate intent into actual knowledge and action.

---

## 🚀 Core Features Powering the Dashboard

### 📊 Personalized Goals & Industrial Assessments
No generic advice here. The app takes user assessment profiles—ranging from personal driving and diet habits to industrial operations and factory resource metrics—and calculates dynamic, specific $CO_2e$ offset targets tailored just for them.

### 🤖 AI Sustainability Coach
Integrated with **Google Gemini**, giving individuals and industrial operators a smart, real-time AI companion to guide them through custom eco-adjustments, compliance questions, and smart efficiency upgrades.

### 🎮 Point-Based Motivation System
To keep engagement high, every sustainable action logged (like switching to eco-HVAC modes or optimization tasks) earns impact points.

### 🏆 Leaderboard
A competitive space where individuals and businesses can see how their sustainability scores stack up against peers, driving community-led and industry-wide climate action.

### 📚 Blogs & Corporate Awareness Articles
A curated climate library packed with educational insights, compliance guidance, and green operational practices to turn intent into actual knowledge.

## 🧮 How It Works

GreenBit uses specialized formulas to convert lifestyle and resource consumption metrics into an aggregated Carbon Dioxide Equivalent ($CO_2e$). 

$$\text{Total Emissions } (CO_2e) = \sum (\text{Resource/Activity Data} \times \text{Emission Factor})$$

* **Personal Tier:** Tracks transport ($\text{km} \times \text{fuel factor}$), electricity ($\text{kWh} \times \text{grid intensity}$), and dietary choices.
* **Industrial Tier:** Aggregates factory resource metrics, machinery runtime, and commercial HVAC load cycles to output enterprise-level mitigation strategies.
